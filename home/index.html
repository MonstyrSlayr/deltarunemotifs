Hello noble Deltarune fan

This is a fanmade, unofficial website I'm working on documenting Undertale and Deltarune leitmotifs.

Undertale and Deltarune are video games with songs composed by Toby Fox.

These games have a multitude of songs with many, many motifs.

They can be difficult to spot and hard to understand, so this site plans on documenting them all!

Motifs can also be very subjective!! Do not take this site as the end all be all for motifs.
